Semantic Structure of HTML5

Header

Headers are like headline for a particular "Section", "Article" or a particular grouping of data.

Article

Articles are a group of data or content with relevency which can be independently used or distributed.

Section

Sections are a group of data or content with a central theme.

Footer

Footers are what appear at the end of the data or content in a page.
